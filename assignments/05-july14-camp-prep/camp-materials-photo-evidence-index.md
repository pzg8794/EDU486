## Evidence Limits

- A photograph confirms that an item was present when photographed; it does not confirm current availability, ownership, reservation, cleanliness, completeness, or working condition.
- Visible quantities are minimums, not guaranteed totals.
- Boxes and cases may be empty or may contain a different item than their labels suggest.
- Several tools are specialist or instructor-controlled equipment and are not automatically available for camper use.
- Exact A/B/C storage locations require a physical check because the image sequence does not reliably preserve room transitions.
