## Template

The journal uses the actual document conventions from Piter's [EDE448 Sensory Walk LaTeX Package](https://github.com/pzg8794/EDE448-Sensory_Walk_LaTeX_Package): the Warner title page, red rules and section headings, blue purpose panel, metadata table, centered interpretive claim, framework paragraph, narrow-margin body typography, blue/gray evidence table, APA citation style, and plain numbered pages. The Module 2 content and bibliography remain original to EDU486.

## Reflection Throughline

The journal argues that inclusive rigor is not an accommodation added after a lesson is built. It is the work of making the intellectual path visible, treating youth identities and places as knowledge, preserving evidence limits, and designing several equally rigorous ways for learners to participate and act. Each principle connects Module 2 scholarship to a concrete decision in the Invisible Invaders camp.

## Evidence Used

The reflection was developed from the current Heather Table, syllabus and Module 2 prompt, the assigned Module 2 readings, the July 14-21 public transcripts, the Invisible Invaders operations guide and daily plans, youth and staff feedback from July 21, and Piter's earlier Puzzle Plan/EQUITAS, UDL, neurodivergence, chronic illness, and identity-safe design work. Copyrighted readings and Blackboard materials remain in the ignored `_local-course-materials/` source archive and are not republished here.
